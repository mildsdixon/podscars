# Podscars Admin Directions

## Admin Login

Go to:

https://thepodscarawards.com/admin

If you are not signed in, the site will send you to:

https://thepodscarawards.com/admin/login

Use the approved admin account for the Podscars Supabase project. Keep the password private and do not share it publicly.

## What You Can Manage

From the admin dashboard, you can:

- Review submitted nominations.
- Approve, reject, or manage nomination records.
- Update award categories.
- Edit homepage “How It Works” text.
- Open or close public nomination access.
- Upload the homepage banner image.
- Upload the five homepage advertising slideshow images.
- View live submission totals.

## Advertising Images

Homepage banner image should be:

- 717 x 223 pixels
- JPG or PNG
- This appears under the homepage intro copy

Homepage slideshow ad images should be:

- 1200 x 628 pixels
- JPG or PNG
- One image per ad spot
- Five active ad spots for the homepage slideshow

To add or replace ads:

1. Go to the admin dashboard.
2. Find the Advertising section.
3. Upload a JPG or PNG for the Homepage banner, or upload one image for each slideshow ad spot.
4. Save the changes.
5. Refresh the homepage to confirm the banner or slideshow updated.

## Public Pages

Visible public pages:

- Home
- Podcast Nominations
- Streaming Nominations

Hidden from public:

- Voting page
- Planning page

The Planning page is admin-only. Public visitors who try to open it will be redirected to admin login.

## Nomination Deadline

Nominations close Monday, August 17, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET.

## Quick Checks

After making changes:

1. Open https://thepodscarawards.com/
2. Confirm the homepage looks correct.
3. Open the nomination pages and confirm forms are working.
4. Check admin dashboard for new submissions.
5. Refresh after saving ad or text changes.
